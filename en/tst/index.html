Master class at Shibuya

  • Address 2 F, 30-2 Sakuragaokachō, Shibuya-ku, Tōkyō-to
  • Date February 21st, 2015, From 1:30PM to 4:30PM

Dear friends!

We invite you to Pysanky workshop. In our classes you will learn about Ukrainian tradition of 'batik' style egg painting art passed down for thousands of years. We will introduce you to techniques of traditional Ukrainian art and will help you to craft your own pysanka.

Those taking part first time will learn the basics, but if you have participated in the previous classes you can further improve your skills. Every one can create unique present talisman for your friends, loved and relatives.

We will be happy to see you on February 21st for out first master class in a series, together with your friends and children.

If you are interested in taking part, please make a reservation by contacting us on following phone number 09093183293 Koval'ova Natalya, 09081190423 Lysenko Natalya.
